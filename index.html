Computer Engineering

What is Computer Engineering?

Computer Engineering is a 3-year with 5-semesters degree which teaches a candidate about every aspect of Computer Engineering.

  • It includes a detailed study of coding languages, along with various other important concepts on Computer Architecture, Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, etc.
  • It includes a detailed study of major programming languages like C, C++, Java and Python. Along with the software subjects, this course will also include hardware subjects like Analog and Digital Electronics.
  • This course also includes compulsory internships and live projects, where a candidate would have to perform software operations on their own. This gives them the opportunity to learn about the subject under an industry environment.

C.M.E IN OUR COLLEGE

Our College granting admission through POLYCET generally set minimum eligibility criteria of 35% aggregate marks. The other colleges generally set the minimum aggregate marks criteria between 50%-65%.
In Our College Every year, more than a 130 candidates contest to get admission to this course. The admission to Computer Engineering course is granted on the basis of Entrance Exams. The most important State level entrance exams for this course are POLYCET, while there are many other state-level and Institute level entrance exams as well.
Once you got the admission, as already said this is 3yrs long course you will learn many things about computer science Some of the popular subjects included in this 3-year long course are Data Structure , Computer Architecture, Database Management Systems, Operating Systems, Microprocessors etc...
